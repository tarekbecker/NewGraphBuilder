BMG is a leading full service digital agency, specializing in China, Japan and Korea Markets. We provide solutions that harness Big Data Scale to improve marketing ROI in digital media across search, social, mobile and display. We are different because of the talent we hire and because we believe in exceptional service for our clients. We enable our Clients and Agency partners to increase market share, improve operational efficiencies, reach evolving ROI goals and drive incremental revenue. BMG is a Tencent Holdings Partner, a Naver Certified Agency, Google Certified Partner, a Yahoo Partner and a Key Agency of Baidu and Qihoo 360. BMG operates out of its offices in Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing, Tokyo and Sydney.

Specialties

Conversion Rate Optimization, Digital Strategy and Solutions, Media Buying and Planning on China, Japan and Korea Digita Media, Social Media Marketing, Real Time Bidding, Technology Integration
