Xplenty

Data Integration-as-a-Service

Xplenty’s Data Integration-as-a-Service harnesses the power of Hadoop to provide an easy-to-use, scalable ETL service that makes it a snap to prepare your structured and semi-structured data for analytics. With Xplenty, you can process data in your favorite cloud storage, such as AWS, Rackspace, Google or IBM SoftLayer. Xplenty can join data from multiple sources, including MySQL, PostgreSQL and MongoDB, and provides a wide range of out-of-the-box data transformation tasks, from simple sorting and aggregation to sophisticated functions and data manipulations.  When finished, place your transformed data in analytics stores such as AWS Redshift, SAP HANA, or back in your favorite relational database, cloud storage or NoSQL data store.
