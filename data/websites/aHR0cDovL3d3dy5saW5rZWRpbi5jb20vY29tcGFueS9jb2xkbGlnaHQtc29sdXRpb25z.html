ColdLight makes the Neuron platform. Neuron makes everything intelligent.

Neuron is an artificial intelligence-based learning technology specifically designed to power more intelligent software, devices, and decisions.

Through a unique use of proprietary artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning technology, Neuron has the ability to automate the work of thousands of data scientists and statisticians working together – in a fraction of the time. It builds highly-customized, highly-accurate data models by using some data to train itself, and some to validate accuracy. Neuron can continuously deliver it's findings to users, applications, and devices – all without any programming - ever.

As data models degrade over time - Neuron uses a constant learning technology to continuously update models so that its predictions and findings remain highly accurate over time.

Organizations use Neuron to create centralized intelligence hubs that constantly and consistently feed predictive information to other applications, devices and technologies throughout their organization.

Developers use the Neuron API library to easily embed the power of Neuron in their products - making them instantly more intelligent than ever thought possible.

Specialties

Machine Learning, Business Intelligence, Advanced Analytics, Data Science, Internet of Things, Connected Device Analysis, Big Data, Open Data, Healthcare Analytics, Population Profiling, Predictive Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Application Platform
