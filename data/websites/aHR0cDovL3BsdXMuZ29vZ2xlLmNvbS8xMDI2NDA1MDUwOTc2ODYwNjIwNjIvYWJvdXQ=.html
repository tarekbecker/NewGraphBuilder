Über mich
Roundforest is a global technology company developing and providing various online products and solutions used by millions of users around the world.

The web today is suffering from information overload. Across various industries - getting a simple answer or reaching relevant information can be tedious, frustrating and sometimes impossible. Information is scattered across the web, and research is difficult and time-consuming. People feel lost. 

At Roundforest, we are targeting the worst pain-points and solve them. We transform existing & exhausting processes into friendly solutions using big data analysis and sophisticated technologies combined together with thoughtful and clean UX.
