BellaDati is complete agile data analytics tool in the hands of business user. Pure web-technology based. Empower business users to turn virtually any-size and type of data into profits. Our products are BellaDati Agile BI, Analytics Apps, Mobile BI & Data Platform. BellaDati advantage is.

- Highly business user focused
- Agile BI. Pure web-tech. Complete
- Cloud or On-premise version
- Social network for business data discovery
- Reports are created real-time not developed
- Unstructured and structured data analysis
- Industry Analytic Apps & +100 data connectors
- Native Mobile BI app for iOS & Android
- Data analytics platform SDK & APIs
- Standalone or complement of traditional SAP, Cognos, SAS BIs

Our famous customers are RedBull, Korean Telecom, New World Resources and other companies from retail, market research, insurance, manufacturing or hospitality services.

Specialties

Big Data Insights, Agile Business Intelligence, Mobile Business Intelligence, Mobile Device Dashboards, Analytical Services, Big Data Integration Services, Agile BI business user focus
