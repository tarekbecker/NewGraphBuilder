Beekeeper Data (Beekeeper) provides a licensed, collaborative, software platform that lets executives and non-technical employees quickly ask many different questions of complex data. Beekeeper’s first product is rolling out to paid private beta customers during Q4 2014. The company was incorporated in Delaware in June 2014 and is based in Madison, WI.
