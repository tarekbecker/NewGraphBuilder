ONDORE SCOUT

Discover what people are saying about brands, industries, products and marketing campaigns across all major social media channels and the web; understand how your reputation compares to that of the competition; and identify who is most influential about topics that are important to you.

Ondore Scout allows you to easily track what people are saying about your brand, industry or campaign without being overwhelmed by the irrelevant data and noise inherent in the web. Listen in the way that is most relevant to you, including: sentiment, source, influence, location and more.

Measuring results in the social web is key to defining and innovating your digital strategy. Focus on the metrics most important to you by creating customized KPI’s, and you upload your own data to correlate with the social media and web metrics you’re tracking in Ondore Scout. Completely customizable dashboards allow you to organize information in the way that is most useful for you.
