Boxever provides Customer Intelligence and Personalisation solutions to the travel industry. We enable organisations build a scalable, cost-effective single customer view that is used to power 1-1 marketing and merchandising across each customer touchpoint. This enhances the customer experience, improves conversion and increases transaction size by relating to each customer individually.

For more information, visit our website www.boxever.com, follow @boxever or email us hello@boxever.com

Specialties

Travel, Marketing, Big Data, Real-Time Decisioning, Customer Intelligence, Personalisation, Customer Data Management, Customer Insights, Single Customer View, Customer Analytics
