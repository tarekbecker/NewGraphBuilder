We're a single application that helps you get any data into Hadoop, bring it together, analyze it, and visualize it as quickly and easily as possible. No coding required. Everything in Datameer is self-service and intuitive, from our wizard-based data integration, to a spreadsheet with point-and-click analytics, to our blank canvas to for building custom visualizations. We were founded by Hadoop veterans in 2009, we sit on top of any Hadoop distribution, and we scale quickly and easily from a laptop to thousands of nodes.

Specialties

Big Data Analytics, Hadoop Analytics, Big Data Discovery, Data Integration, Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Analytic Apps, Data Management
