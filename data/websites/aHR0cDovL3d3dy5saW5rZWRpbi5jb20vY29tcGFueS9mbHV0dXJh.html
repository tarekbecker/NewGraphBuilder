Flutura was incorporated in February 2012. Flutura is a butterfly in Albanian. We drew inspiration from the evolution of a caterpillar to butterfly which epitomizes nature's greatest transformation in the biological world. It is symbolic of our aspiration to transform decisioning processes within organisations and enable disruptive outcomes.

The flutura team has over a decade been strategic advisors to several Fortune 500 organizations worldwide and been part of several transformational Information, Analytics and Process Innovation programs.

We believe a fundamental shift has to and will happen over the next decade in terms of adoption of BI and Analytics worldwide. We believe ever bleeding DW and BI initiatives will come to an end, we believe organizations will look for rapidly expanding and scaling analytics initiatives, we believe companies will more closely watch outcomes than outputs and insights. Organizations will also be challenged with the worsening trend of perpetual talent shortage worldwide.

Flutura is well positioned to intercept this shift. Our next generation WINGS methodology and Cerebra suite of analytics products unlocks dollar impacting frictions for our customers to realize disruptive outcomes rapidly.

Specialties

Big Data, Business Intelligence, Analytics, Machine Learning, Utility, High Tech, Oil and Gas, Industrial Manufacturing, Safety Risk Management, M2M, IOT
