Data Archiving and Backup
Archive large volumes of data from Oracle into portable format files with high performance, low system overhead and reduced storage needs. The archived data can be easily and quickly accessed at any point in time.

WisdomForce enables unloading data from tablespaces, residing on regular disk, SAN or ASM into text files on NFS or NAS mounted devices. more
