Data Warehouse Automation

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

BIReady is the market leader in Data Warehouse Automation software. Use BIReady to drive down costs and speed up your Data Warehouse development projects. BIReady’s Model-driven Data Warehouse generation engine takes care of the hard work for you using best practices and a stable architecture that you can manage and tweak with ease.

 

Data Warehouse Automation has become vitally important in recent years as Data Warehousing best-practices have matured and standardized approaches have emerged. Today, building a quality Data Warehouse by hand, while fun, is just too slow and expensive for most organizations today. This is why Automation has become so important, and where BIReady delivers unparalleled values.

About:

 

BIReady is the vendor of a unique software product that automatically designs, generates and populates Enterprise Data Warehouses and Data Marts, based on the organization’s Business Model. This awesome product, named after the company, replaces intensive manual labor. BIReady even generates and executes the ETL code!

 

BIReady handles the lifecycle of Data Warehouses and Data Vault.. from mapping to source systems, generation of ETL, designing and generating and loading the normalized Data Warehouse and star-schema Data Marts/ Data Vault, maintenance of history and surrogate keys, change-data capture and logging. These processes are scalable, stable and automated. Using BIReady, your Data Warehouse is taken care of, surfacing the data you need, and supporting your favourite BI reporting tools.
