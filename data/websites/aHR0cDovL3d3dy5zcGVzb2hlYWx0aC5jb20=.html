Frequently Asked Questions

What is CurityMD?

CurityMD is a new product launched by SpeSo Health an early stage Healthcare IT company that is developing an online second opinion and educational platform that serves patients with rare and complex conditions. We are working to globalize medical expertise and empower these patients and their families to find answers faster, and achieve better medical outcomes more with less stress.

We help patients with rare, complex and severe conditions find the specialists and hospitals that are best-suited to help them manage their specific case. We also help connect patients with leading specialists nationwide and share their health records to receive an online second opinion.

How does it work?

Identify the right expert based on a condition

Enter a rare or complex condition into our search system that will cover over 6,000 rare conditions. The CurityMD Analysis tools then identify the specialists and hospitals that are most experienced at treating that condition. We do this using objective, unbiased data sources related to research, quality, capabilities, funding, influence and productivity.

(Coming Soon): Secure online 2nd opinion

The SpeSo platform will soon allow you to connect with the expert hospital experts identified for your condition, by securely transferring medical information to a leading expert center. Specialists at that center then provide a second opinion to the patient, all delivered online.

Do I need to provide information to search?

We want to make our information available to as many patients as possible, as quickly as possible. That's why we have launched global access to CurityMD without requiring any user information. For the future, we invite all of our users to submit their email and stay up to date as we build new features into the site.

In the future, we will let users save searches and communicate directly with experts through a simple, private account profile. With a SpeSo Profile, a user can customize their search: location information, treatments of interest, automatic alerts for clinical trials and new research nearby - all information that can help us find you top-class experts for treating patients just like you.

Does CurityMD cost anything to use?

The CurityMD patient access service is free for all patients!

Can I communicate with experts online?

Today, SpeSo is working with hospitals and physicians to set up pilot programs to help patients communicate with qualified experts online. We are also building a secure platform to allow CurityMD users to share personal health records with an expert health institution and receive a second opinion within a matter of days. Empowering patients with SpeSo's online access programs can eliminate long waits, costly travel, and inconvenient hotel stays.

Who are CurityMD's Supporters?

SpeSo Health, and their product CurityMD, are an independent company. We serve patients first. We believe that empowering rare and complex condition communities with better information can be both a valuable service and a successful business. We are fortunate to have supporters at the frontier of medicine and technology.
